01 Intro
2 min
"Thanks for taking the time today. I want to spend about 25 minutes learning about your business, what's eating time, and where AI might actually help. If it looks like there is a fit, I can explain the AI Workflow Setup Session as a practical next step. Sound good?"
  • Set the agenda out loud so they know what to expect
  • Keep it relaxed — conversation, not a hard sales pitch
  • You talk ~30% of the time. Listen more than you speak.
  • The goal is fit, not a proposal.
02 Business Background
5 min
  • What does your business do, and who do you serve?
  • How many people are on your team?
  • How long have you been operating?
  • How do you primarily generate revenue — project-based, recurring, product sales, appointments, service visits, or something else?
  • What does a normal busy week look like?

03 Pain Points
8 min
  • Walk me through a typical week — what's eating the most time?
  • What does your team do over and over that feels like it should be easier?
  • Where do things fall through the cracks?
  • What gets forgotten, delayed, copied by hand, or retyped between systems?
  • What has actually gone wrong because of a manual process?
  • If you could fix one thing about how your business operates, what would it be?

It's a 60-90 minute working session where we set up your AI Operating System folder structure, capture your business context, build the initial tool inventory, and identify the first connector that would make the system more useful. That session is $150, and it is meant to be a low-risk first step before we talk about connector setup, automation projects, dashboards, or retainers."
