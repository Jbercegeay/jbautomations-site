About

I'm Johnny Bercegeay — a systems analyst and software builder with over a decade of experience studying how businesses actually operate. I've worked with operational teams and small-business workflows, spending years at the intersection of operations and technology: mapping workflows, identifying bottlenecks, and designing systems that fix them for real.

I started JB Automations because I kept seeing the same pattern: business owners who knew AI could help them, but had no clear idea where to start. The tools are real. The ROI is real. What's missing is someone who understands your specific workflows well enough to cut through the hype and tell you exactly what's worth doing — and what isn't.

I don't do generic demos or vendor-pushed recommendations. I sit down with you, learn how your business actually runs, and help set up the foundation before recommending a bigger build. Sometimes the next step is a workflow automation. Sometimes it is simply organizing your business context so AI can finally give useful answers. The goal is always practical impact, not impressive-looking technology.

I build things that ship, not things that sit in slide decks — and I stay involved after launch to make sure they keep working.
