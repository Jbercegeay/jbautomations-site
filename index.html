What We Offer

Four ways to work
with us.

Every engagement starts with the discovery call. From there, you choose the level of involvement that makes sense for your business and budget.

🗺
AI Strategy Roadmap

A formal written document that maps your current workflows, identifies AI opportunities, recommends specific tools (Claude, Codex, n8n, and others), and prioritizes implementation by ROI. Yours to keep and act on — with or without us.

The most common first engagement after a discovery call.

🎓
Training & Workshops

Hands-on sessions that teach your team to use AI tools effectively — prompt engineering, Claude and Codex workflows, building internal automations, and how to evaluate new AI tools as they emerge. Half-day or full-day sessions.

Ideal for teams ready to adopt AI but unsure how to start.

⚡
Implementation

We build what the roadmap recommends — AI automations, custom software and web apps, AI agents, and business websites. Project-based, with clear scope and deliverables agreed upfront.

For businesses ready to move from strategy to execution.

🤝
Ongoing Advisory Retainer

Monthly access to an AI advisor who knows your business. Regular check-ins, answering questions as they come up, evaluating new tools, and making sure your AI investments keep delivering. AI moves fast — this keeps you ahead of it.

For businesses that want a long-term partner, not a one-time project.

AI Strategy Workflow Analysis n8n Python Next.js Convex Systems Design

I'm a systems analyst and software builder based in Alabama. I've spent years studying how businesses actually operate — where the friction lives, where hours get wasted, and where a well-designed system changes everything.

I started JB Automations because I kept seeing the same pattern: business owners who knew AI could help them, but had no clear idea where to start. The tools are real. The ROI is real. What's missing is someone who understands your specific workflows well enough to cut through the hype and tell you what's actually worth doing.

The discovery call exists because the right answer is different for every business. A manufacturing shop has different opportunities than a law office. A five-person team needs a different approach than a fifty-person team. The audit isn't a product demo — it's a real analysis of your situation.

My first consumer product, Home Desk, is live and serving real households today. I build things that ship, not things that sit in slide decks.
